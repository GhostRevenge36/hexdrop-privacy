HexDrop Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Data Collection

HexDrop collects no data of any kind. The extension does not transmit, store, log, or share any information about you, your files, or your browsing activity.

File Processing

Files you open in HexDrop are read directly by your browser and processed entirely within your local browser tab. No file contents, file names, or metadata are sent to any server or third party at any time.

Permissions

HexDrop requests the tabs permission solely to open the full-screen viewer in a new browser tab when you click the extension icon. This permission is not used to read, monitor, or access any tab's URL, content, or browsing history. No tab data of any kind is collected, stored, or transmitted.

Network Activity

HexDrop never connects to the internet. It contains no analytics, no tracking pixels, no crash reporting, no update checks, and no third-party SDKs of any kind. The extension works completely offline.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at hexdrop.support@gmail.com. We will respond within 48 hours.
